Boringless is a website that provides a wide variety of activities to do during any spare time. We have all found ourselves free from responsibilities and ready to play and have fun, but with no ideas coming to mind. This is where boringless takes place. We help you get ideas on things to do based off of 4 categories: Social, Outdoors, Education and Dates. Users are required to complete a basic sign up process and select a category. Once a selection is made, a list of activities will be generated to choose from. You choose an activity, we give you a few instructions on how to complete it, and then you go and try it out. Having fun was never that easy!
